Our History

SkillBuilder was born from a desire to give educators a way to create quality education experiences over the internet. From its humble beginnings in 2023, SkillBuilder has constantly evolved through our commitment to pedagogy, open source philosophy, and a collaborative global community of like-minded educators and technologists.
