Welcome to Skillbuilder, the online employee training app designed to help your workforce develop the skills they need to succeed. Our platform provides a comprehensive suite of training courses that cover a wide range of topics, from technical skills to soft skills and leadership development.

Our courses are created by your instructor and delivered in an engaging and interactive format that combines videos, readings, quizzes, and hands-on exercises. With Skillbuilder, you can access high-quality training anytime, anywhere, and on any device.

The training courses are designed to help you develop the skills you need to succeed in their roles and advance your careers. Our courses cover topics such as:

  • Technical Skills: Programming languages, database management, software development, and more.

  • Soft Skills: Communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and other essential skills for the modern workplace.

  • Leadership Development: Management skills, coaching and mentoring, strategic thinking, and more.Technical Skills: Programming languages, database management, software development, and more.

In addition to our training courses, we offer a range of features to help you manage your training program and track your employees' progress. Our platform includes

  • Course Catalog: Browse our course catalog and sign up for new courses.

  • My Courses: View your enrolled courses, track your progress, and view upcoming deadlines.

  • Resources: Access additional resources related to your training, such as articles, videos, and discussion forums.

  • Profile: View and edit your personal information, such as your name, contact information, and job title.

  • Support: Contact our support staff or access help documentation if you have any issues or questions about the app or your training.
